2024 Roadmap:


September Update:
It's been a while since I've updated the website.. SORRY!

The Vidsomewhere was a Huge Success! We had some new and returning participants, and we were almost overwhelmed with the quantity of submissions this year!.

I'm looking forward to what we can do next year!

As for the Sword Wizard Project, this is placed onto indefinite hold.
I had written and rewritten some rough drafts, but none felt good enough. I've taken some time to reflect and Overall, I feel like I need to develope my narrative skills on smaller projects first.

Artisan Monsters is still under development, but I am extending the deadline to Q4 of 2025.
I could rush through and spaghettie my way through development to get ver 1 done by the end of the year.
Instead, I'm electing slow down development to allow me to better balance the stress of programming against my other life obligations.

Finally, I have a Wizard Avatar that I'm developing for VRChat.
Using OBS and the VRCs built in recording features, I can digitize myself into various environments. I'm hopful that I can start making short films soon!


Background:

Almost 2 Years ago I quit my job to focus on my creative career. I learned quickly that I was not adequately equipped to manage a business. A few months later I ended up finding another job as an Assembler, I learned a lot about Manufacturing and Dignity of Work.
Being the highly motivated person that I am, I quickly rose through the Assembler Career Ladder, and applied for a position to work with robots as a Technician.

Finding myself now on an Engineering Path, I had to learn a lot of new skills, develop better habits around time management, and learn how to self motivate just to name a few. Recently I've started to feel more confident in myself at work, and my home life has started to balance back out.

My growth as a creative internet person is still on going, and I hope you can enjoy the journey! (Or at least the content along the way.)
