Space Egg Survivor:
Two Stick Shooter, Survivors-Like, on Hiatus.

You are a Spaceborne Egg-Creature, and the most delicious thing known to the wider space community.
The Game starts as a Two-Stick Shooter, but as you grow in experiance you evolve new powers and control systems.
Select from a list of "Powers" randomly generated by combining an Effect and an Input. Not only do you end up with different optimal builds each run, the way you play the game changes each run aswell! Taking Replayability to the next level.

This game is on Hiatus due to my migration away from Games Engines, but I really love the Concept of "Learning to play the game, everytime I play the game." There's something magical about learning something new, and I Hope to return to this Idea very soon.
